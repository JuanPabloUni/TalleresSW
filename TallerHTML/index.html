About Super Mario

Super Mario is a video game series developed by Nintendo that follows the adventures of an Italian plumber named Mario as he navigates through various worlds to rescue Princess Peach from the antagonist Bowser. The first game, Super Mario Bros., was released in 1985 and has since spawned numerous sequels and spin-offs.

Classic Mario comes to the big screen! Familiar characters, worlds and music all around!.
